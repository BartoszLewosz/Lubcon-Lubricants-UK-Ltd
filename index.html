Lubcon Lubricants UK Ltd

We are a worldwide operating lubricant manufacturer developing, producing and distributing high-grade greases, oils, pastes, sprays and lubrication systems for nearly all industrial applications and manufacturing sectors.

Products

Our product range includes high and low-temperature lubricants, assembly pastes, gear, hydraulic, chain and compressor oils, bonded coatings and silicone lubricants. For all applications in the food, beverage and pharmaceutical industry, we offer a wide range of H1 (and 3H) certified lubricants.

  • Aerosols
  • Cleaners and Solvents
  • Greases
  • Oils
  • Pastes
  • Lubrication Systems

Services

With your collaboration we get to the root cause of premature failures, compile solutions and support you throughout the entire improvement process.
